# Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed (RACI) Matrix 
- Coined in Business Process, ITIL, DevOps

| Letter | Full Term     | Meaning                     | Core Responsibility                                  |
|--------|----------------|-----------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| R      | Responsible    | The doer                    | Executes the task; may include multiple individuals   |
| A      | Accountable    | The owner                   | Ultimately answerable; approves and signs off; only one |
| C      | Consulted      | The advisor                 | Provides input or expertise; two‑way communication    |
| I      | Informed       | The notified party          | Kept updated on progress; one‑way communication       |
